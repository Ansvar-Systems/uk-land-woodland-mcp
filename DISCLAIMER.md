# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides land and woodland management data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional legal, planning, or land management advice. Regulations, thresholds, and grant rates are based on published UK government sources, but individual circumstances vary. Requirements differ by local planning authority, devolved administration, and site-specific designations (SSSI, conservation area, TPO, etc.). Always consult your local planning authority, Natural England, or the Forestry Commission before taking action.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **Hedgerow Regulations 1997** -- Crown Copyright, UK Government
- **Forestry Act 1967 (as amended)** -- Crown Copyright, UK Government
- **Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981** -- Crown Copyright, UK Government
- **Countryside and Rights of Way Act 2000** -- Crown Copyright, UK Government
- **Commons Act 2006** -- Crown Copyright, UK Government
- **England Woodland Creation Offer (EWCO)** -- Forestry Commission
- **Environmental Impact Assessment (Forestry) Regulations 1999** -- Crown Copyright

All data is used under the [Open Government Licence v3](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/).

## Currency

Land management regulations change. Grant rates, thresholds, and processes are updated by government departments. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
